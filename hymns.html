HYMNS

  • 1 Now that the daylight fills the sky
  • 2 O Jesu, Lord of life and grace
  • 3 Glory to thee who safe hast kept.
  • 4 New every morning is the love
  • 5 My Father, for another night
  • 6 Come, Holy Ghost, who ever one
  • 7 Awake, my soul, and with the sun
  • 8 O Trinity, most blessed Light
  • 9 Abide with me
  • 10 O Saviour Lord, to thee we pray
  • 11 As now the sun’s declining rays
  • 12 Sun of my Soul, thou Saviour dear
  • 13 Before the ending of the day
  • 14 Glory to thee, my God, this night
  • 15 Hail, gladdening Light
  • 16 Now the day is over
  • 17 May the grace of Christ our Saviour
  • 18 The day thou gavest, Lord, is ended
  • 19 On this day, the first of days
  • 20 Blest Creator of the Light
  • 21 Lo! He comes with clouds decsending
  • 22 Creator of the starry height
  • 23 On Jordan’s bank the Baptist’s cry
  • 24 O heavenly Word, eternal Light
  • 25 O come, O come, Emmanuel
